About Us

Spotshare is composed of a team that are enthousiast on collecting postcards because of their sentimental value and artistic sense.

Spotshare is a postcard memorabiblia social network. The main purpose of the application is for you to go out into the world, explore and get rewarded for it by collecting amazing digital poscards themed with the locations that you visited. After collection you can customize and share it with the world! (Or you friends!).

Features


  • Collect Postcard Themed with the Visited Location

  • Exlore new Locations that appear in the application

  • View Collected Postcards

  • Personalize Collected Postcards

  • Share the Postcards with the World (or your Friends)

  • Categorize Collected Postcards

  • Dispose of Unwanted Postcards

  • View Postcards published by other

  • View your progress in the way of a Collection Journal
